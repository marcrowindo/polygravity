FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Polygravity an open source technology?

Yes it is. However, code is only released after development on each protocol (see technology section) is completed and has gone through internal auditing. To stay updated on code releases we ask you to monitor our technology section.

Will Polygravity ever ICO? / Can I purchase Polygravity tokens?

No cryptocurrency is required to interact with Polygravity's network and there are no plans to introduce it. In Polygravity's view cryptocurrency would produce much more friction than added benefits if introduced. As a result of this no ICO is foreseen and no currency related to Polygravity can be obtained.

Please be weary of potential schams that pretend to represent Polygravity.

How can I use Polygravity?

At this point in time Polygravity focuses solely on C2B / B2C / B2B transactions. If you are a business that is interested in our technology simply get in touch with us via our contact forms or send us an email to info@polygravity.io.

Our product roadmap predicts that we are able to allow for peer to peer transactions within the next two years of development.

If you can't find the question you are looking for, just drop us a message and we'll get back to you in no time.
